🤝 Community Guidelines

These guidelines apply to MemoRebel's Friends feature — adding friends, sharing routes, and the activity feed. Following them keeps MemoRebel welcoming for everyone.

✅ Be respectful

Treat other explorers the way you'd want to be treated. No harassment, hate speech, threats, or discriminatory language in friend requests, shared routes, or the activity feed.

🔒 Respect privacy

Don't share another person's location, photos, or personal information without their consent. Only add people as friends if you actually know them or they've agreed to connect.

🚫 No spam or abuse

Don't send unsolicited friend requests in bulk, repeatedly message people who've declined you, or use the activity feed to advertise unrelated products or services.

⚠️ No harmful content

Never share content that promotes self-harm, violence, illegal activity, or content that could endanger yourself or others.

🚩 Reporting & blocking

If someone makes you uncomfortable, you can Block them (they can no longer friend or contact you) or Report them (sends a private report to MemoRebel for review) from their profile in the Friends screen. Blocking and reporting are independent — you can do either or both.

⚖️ Enforcement

Accounts that repeatedly violate these guidelines may have their social features (friends, sharing, activity feed) restricted. This never affects your personal data, memories, or progress, which always stay on your device.
