What you can do

📍

Pin Memories

Drop a memory at any location with a note or photo, and watch it come alive again when you walk past.

🧠

Smart Study

Turn any topic into a walkable study trail — AI-generated capsules with quizzes, paced to your walk.

🧘

Calm Trails

Mindfulness, calm, and peaceful-mind walking trails to reset your headspace, one stop at a time.

🌱

Habit Garden

Plant habits at physical locations and grow them by checking in — visualized as a living garden.

🎯

Daily Quests

Three simple daily goals — walk, solve, log a mood — that keep your streak alive and your mind engaged.

👥

Friends & Sharing

Add friends by a unique MemoRebel ID, share routes and achievements, and see their activity feed.

Built for explorers who learn on foot

MemoRebel combines GPS, spaced repetition, and AI-generated study content to make walking time count double — exercise for your body and your memory. All personal data (memories, mood logs, routes) stays on your device unless you choose to sign in and sync.
